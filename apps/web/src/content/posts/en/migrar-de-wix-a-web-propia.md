Your business needs a professional online presence to attract and retain customers. Using website builders like Wix or templates can be a good starting point, but as your business grows, you may find that these solutions have limitations. A custom website can provide the flexibility and control you need to effectively represent your brand and achieve your goals.

## What are the limitations of Wix and templates?
Wix and templates are designed to be easy to use and require little to no coding knowledge. However, this ease of use comes at a cost. You are limited to the features and designs provided by the platform or template, which can make it difficult to stand out from the competition. Additionally, you may have limited control over the user experience and the ability to make changes to the site.

## Benefits of a custom website
A custom website can provide many benefits, including:
* More control over the design and layout of the site
* The ability to create a unique user experience
* Greater flexibility to make changes and updates
* Improved search engine optimization (SEO)
* Better integration with other tools and systems

With a custom website, you can create a site that is tailored to your specific business needs and goals. You can choose the features and functionality that are most important to you, and create a design that reflects your brand and values.

## Improved SEO
A custom website can also improve your search engine optimization (SEO). With a custom site, you have more control over the code and structure of the site, which can help improve your search engine rankings. You can also optimize your site for specific keywords and phrases, and create high-quality content that attracts and engages your target audience.

## Greater flexibility and scalability
A custom website can also provide greater flexibility and scalability. As your business grows and changes, you can easily make updates and changes to the site to reflect your new needs and goals. You can also add new features and functionality as needed, without being limited by the constraints of a website builder or template.

## How to make the transition
Making the transition from a Wix or template site to a custom website can seem daunting, but it does not have to be. You can start by identifying your goals and needs, and then working with a developer or designer to create a custom site that meets those needs. You can also migrate your existing content to the new site, and make updates and changes as needed.

To get started, consider the following steps:
* Define your goals and objectives for the new site
* Identify your target audience and their needs
* Determine the features and functionality you need
* Work with a developer or designer to create the new site
* Migrate your existing content to the new site
