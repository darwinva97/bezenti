Fitness Center is a neighborhood gym with group classes and in-house trainers. They brought in members almost entirely by word of mouth and walk-ins. They wanted a website that explained plans and schedules and let people sign up without stepping up to the front desk.

## The challenge

The gym was up against large chains that already ranked first on Google. Their audience searches for specific things: "gym near me", "spinning classes", "monthly membership no contract". Without an organized site or a local search presence, that traffic went to competitors. And anyone who did find the old site couldn't see the plans clearly or how to join.

## What we did

We built a website aimed at winning members:

- Membership plans side by side, with price and terms in plain sight.
- A class schedule by day and discipline, easy to read on mobile.
- Trainer profiles to put a face to the team.
- Online sign-up with no trip to the front desk.
- Copy and structure made to turn a visit into a membership.
- Local SEO to show up for "gym near me" and class-based searches.

## How we built it

We started from positioning: we researched the neighborhood's real searches and built the page architecture around them, one per intent (plans, classes, trainers, schedules). The copy gets to the point — what each plan includes, no fine print — because that's where the membership is decided.

On the technical side, a fast site on Cloudflare Pages, AA accessibility and local business structured data with hours and location, tied to the gym's listing. Online sign-up is a short flow, made to be completed from a phone in the waiting room or on the couch.

## Results

With local SEO in place and a clear path to joining, online sign-ups rose and the gym added new members every month. Organic traffic doubled once it started showing up for the searches the chains used to capture.
