El Patrón is a barbershop with a vintage feel: classic cuts, straight-razor shaves and beard trims. They lived off WhatsApp bookings and walk-ins. We built them a website that keeps that character and lets people book online without losing the neighborhood touch.

## The challenge

You come back to a barbershop for the result and the atmosphere. The brand had a good product and a place with personality, but none of that showed online: no clear pricing, no cut gallery, no way to book outside opening hours. Chat bookings flooded the phone and appointments overlapped. The job was to bring the in-shop experience to the screen and tidy up the schedule.

## What we did

Design and build of the site, focused on booking:

- A service menu with pricing (cut, beard, shave, combos).
- A cut gallery that conveys the house style.
- A booking system with barber, service and available time slot.
- WhatsApp reminders and contact with a pre-filled message.
- Location with a map and opening hours.
- Local SEO for searches like "barbershop near me".

## How we built it

The vintage identity called for fonts with character and high-resolution photos, so we watched the weight: optimized images, local fonts and an LCP under a second. We meet AA accessibility so booking is easy on any phone. For technical SEO we added local-business structured data, hours and reviews, a sitemap and metadata. The booking system prevents overlaps and notifies via WhatsApp, so the barber starts the day with the schedule in order.

## Results

The site turned interest into real appointments. Online bookings grew, the schedule filled out better on weekdays, and the fast load made booking from a phone a matter of seconds.
