Pequeños Genios is a daycare that brought in families almost entirely by word of mouth. They wanted a website that reassured parents and let them start enrollment without visiting the center first. This audience decides quickly and distrusts anything that isn't clearly laid out.

## The challenge

Parents choose a daycare with their head and their gut: they need facts (programs, ages, schedules) but also proof that the place is safe and warm. And nearly all of them search from a phone, often "daycare near me." If the site didn't show up in their district or convey trust, the family wouldn't even call.

## What we did

We designed and built the full website, with online enrollment as the goal.

- Programs explained by age range, with schedules and what each one includes.
- A features section: facilities, meals and safety.
- Parent testimonials for social proof.
- A step-by-step online enrollment form.
- A gallery of the spaces so families can see the atmosphere before visiting.
- A colorful, warm design, responsive and mobile-first.

## How we built it

A fast static site on a CDN, loading instantly on mobile because that's where the decision is made. We worked local SEO thoroughly: business listing, structured data for an educational organization, per-program metadata and content aimed at area-intent searches. AA accessibility, which matters for parents browsing one-handed with a baby in their arms: large buttons, high contrast and clear forms. The enrollment form validates at each step and notifies the center instantly.

## Results

Showing up among the top results in their district brought in families who couldn't find them before, which multiplied parent leads. With a fast site and a simple form, more families completed enrollment online instead of stopping at "I'll call later."
