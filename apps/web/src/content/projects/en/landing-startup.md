Startup Tech was launching a new product and needed a page that explained its value in seconds and pushed visitors to sign up. They didn't want a corporate site: they wanted a landing page that turned paid traffic into booked demos.

## The challenge

The product was technical and the audience was mixed: decision-makers who look at the business and technical profiles who look at the detail. The page had to speak to both without diluting the message, and every visit came from paid campaigns, so each click cost money. A slow or confusing landing page burned budget.

## What we did

We designed and built the full landing page around a single goal: requesting a demo.

- A section-by-section structure: hero with the value proposition, benefits, social proof, features and FAQ.
- Copy rewritten to address concrete problems, not a list of loose features.
- A CTA repeated at key scroll points, with a short form.
- Hero and CTA variants ready for A/B testing.
- Event tracking (scroll, CTA clicks, submissions) to optimize campaigns.
- On-page SEO and metadata for complementary organic traffic.

## How we built it

A lightweight stack, no heavy frameworks: semantic HTML, custom CSS and minimal JavaScript. Modern image formats and lazy loading kept LCP below 1.2 s even on mobile over 4G. The page is static and served from a CDN, so it holds up under campaign traffic spikes without degrading. AA accessibility: proper contrast, visible focus and labeled forms. We wired in event analytics and connected the form to the CRM so every lead entered the funnel clean.

## Results

With a fast page and a sharper message, the conversion rate went up and the cost per lead came down compared to the previous landing page. Loading under 1.2 s improved both the experience and the campaigns' Quality Score, which paid less per click.
