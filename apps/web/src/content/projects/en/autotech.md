AutoTech is an auto repair shop covering maintenance, diagnostics and repairs across several car brands. Its customers came by referral and phone; what was missing was a site explaining what they do, for which cars and how to book. We built a website with an industrial look that organizes the services and opens online booking.

## The challenge

People looking for a shop are usually in a hurry and wary: they've had a bad experience or don't know what they'll be charged. The client needed to convey craft and transparency, make clear which brands they handle and let visitors request a quote or appointment without calling. Before this, everything ran through the phone and appointments were written in a notebook.

## What we did

Design and build of the site, focused on booking and quotes:

- A service catalog (maintenance, brakes, suspension, diagnostics).
- A list of supported brands to filter at a glance.
- A quote tool with service type and vehicle details.
- A booking system with available days and times.
- Direct WhatsApp contact with a pre-filled message.
- Local SEO for searches like "auto repair shop near me".

## How we built it

The site loads fast despite the shop photos: we optimized formats, deferred non-critical assets and kept the LCP above standard. We meet AA accessibility —contrast, keyboard, labeled forms— so anyone can book without friction. For technical SEO we added local-business structured data, hours and location, a sitemap and per-service metadata. The quote tool and booking connect to WhatsApp and email, so the shop replies with the vehicle's context and confirms quickly.

## Results

The site turned search traffic into real appointments. Online bookings grew steadily, quotes came in better qualified, and the fast load reduced drop-off on mobile, where most contact begins.
