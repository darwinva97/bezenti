The studio covered weddings, portraits and product, but its work lived scattered across social networks that compress and crop the photos. They needed a portfolio of their own where the image looks as it should and where a client could request a quote without leaving the site.

## The challenge

For a photographer, the website IS the showcase. Loading dozens of high-res images without the page crawling is the underlying problem: a slow gallery scares off exactly the people who came to judge visual quality. And with no clear path to a quote, visitors looked at photos and left without getting in touch.

## What we did

We designed and custom-built a fast, image-first portfolio.

- Masonry gallery that respects each photo's framing
- Lightbox to view large without reloading the page
- Categories by session type (wedding, portrait, product)
- Session quote system with type, date and details
- A restrained design that lets the photos take the lead
- A smooth mobile version to swipe the gallery with a thumb

## How we built it

The key was image performance: modern formats, sizes served per device, progressive lazy loading and placeholders so scrolling stays smooth even with a full gallery. The gallery opens in under a second.

We custom-built the quote form, woven into the flow so requesting a quote feels natural right after seeing the work. Technical SEO with per-category metadata and structured data to capture searches like "wedding photographer." AA accessibility on navigation, contrast and image alt text.

## Results

The gallery opens almost instantly despite the visual weight, visitors spend more time exploring the work, and a solid share end up requesting a quote instead of just looking. The portfolio turns admiration into real inquiries.
