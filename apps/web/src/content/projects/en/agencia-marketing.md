Agencia Digital sells marketing services, but its own site wasn't selling. They needed a website that proved the craft: clear, fast and with an obvious path to contact. We rebuilt it from structure to copy, and set up the campaigns that feed it.

## The challenge

A marketing agency is judged by its own presence. If the site is slow or confusing, the sales pitch collapses on its own. The previous site listed services without context, showed no results and gave visitors no clear reason to reach out. On top of that, all traffic was direct: no SEO and no campaigns bringing in new clients.

## What we did

A full redesign plus the acquisition engine that feeds it:

- Service pages with problem, approach and concrete deliverables.
- A portfolio with real cases and per-project metrics.
- Testimonials and logos to strengthen social proof.
- A contact form segmented by service type.
- Google Ads and Meta Ads campaigns with dedicated landing pages.
- Content SEO to capture bottom-of-funnel searches.

## How we built it

The site puts speed and conversion first: light loads, a clear visual hierarchy and CTAs in every section. We meet AA accessibility and left technical SEO solid —structured data, sitemap, per-page metadata— to sustain organic ranking. Google and Meta campaigns point to specific landing pages with conversion tracking, so every dollar spent can be attributed. SEO and ads work together: organic builds authority over time, ads bring demand today.

## Results

The site went from brochure to acquisition engine. With SEO and campaigns running, monthly leads grew steadily, key pages reached Google's Top 3, and organic traffic multiplied, cutting the dependence on ad spend.
