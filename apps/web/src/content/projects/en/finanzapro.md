FinanzaPro is a financial consulting firm that advises companies and private wealth. Their old site explained little and gave no clear way to get in touch. They needed a website that signaled credibility and turned a visit into a meeting.

## The challenge

Financial consulting sells on trust, and that trust has to show from the first second. The firm fielded scattered enquiries by phone and email, with no filter and no calendar. The team spent time juggling schedules, and many prospects went cold before the first call. They needed a sharper message, services laid out clearly, and a direct way to book.

## What we did

We designed and built the full corporate site:

- Clear service pages: tax advisory, wealth planning, investment and reporting.
- An animated stats block to give context to the firm's track record.
- An integrated booking system: visitors pick a day and time with no email back-and-forth.
- Forms with fields that qualify the lead before the meeting.
- An SEO structure aimed at financial consulting searches by service and area.
- A restrained design, with type hierarchy made for comfortable reading of figures and data.

## How we built it

A lightweight static site deployed on Cloudflare Pages for fast loads and low cost. We optimized images and deferred non-critical work to keep LCP just over a second on mobile. The stats animation fires only when it enters the viewport, so it never slows the first paint.

For accessibility we targeted AA: enough contrast, visible focus, keyboard navigation and proper labels on the forms. Technical SEO covers per-page metadata, organization structured data and a clean sitemap. Booking connects to the team calendar and confirms by email, with no friction.

## Results

With a clearer message and a calendar open straight from the site, the firm doubled the appointments booked and raised the share of qualified leads that reach the meeting. Fast loading helps make sure no one drops off waiting.
