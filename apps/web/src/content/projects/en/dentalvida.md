DentalVida offers orthodontics, implants, cosmetic dentistry and cleanings, but brought in patients almost entirely through word of mouth. They needed a professional website that conveyed trust, showed the team's work and turned a visit into an appointment.

## The challenge

In healthcare, the decision rests on trust. The previous presence didn't convey it: little treatment information, no faces from the team and no easy way to book. Prospective patients looked and left to find another clinic that felt more reassuring before putting their mouth in someone's hands.

## What we did

We designed and built the website to build trust and drive bookings.

- Per-treatment pages with what's included, the process and FAQs
- Medical team profiles with specialty and training
- Patient testimonials to reinforce the decision
- Online appointment system by treatment and time slot
- Clear calls to action on every page
- A foundation ready for measurable acquisition campaigns

## How we built it

We took care of performance: optimized images, a light build and a low LCP on mobile, where most decisions happen. The structure guides the patient from treatment to appointment without detours.

We worked the local SEO with structured clinic data (hours, address, services) and per-treatment content to show up for searches like "orthodontics near me" or "dental implants." We laid the ground for marketing: clear landing pages and measurement in place, so campaigns can be optimized on real leads. AA accessibility on contrast, keyboard and forms.

## Results

Booked appointments grew once reserving was easy and trust was reinforced, campaign leads doubled on a measurable base, and the clinic gains visibility in local searches by treatment. The site works to capture patients, not just inform them.
